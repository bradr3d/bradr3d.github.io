TaskFrame is the definitive app for managing tasks with intelligent timing.

Whether it's a work deadline, a personal reminder, or a recurring habit, TaskFrame understands when tasks should appear and helps you focus on what matters most at the right time. Say goodbye to overwhelming to-do lists and hello to a smart, intuitive app that shows you the right tasks when you need them.

Smart Timing

TaskFrame's core innovation is intelligent timing. Unlike traditional task managers that show everything at once, TaskFrame understands when tasks should appear and helps you stay focused on what's relevant right now.

  • Tasks appear exactly when you need them, not all at once
  • Start and due dates ensure tasks show up at the perfect time
  • Recurring tasks automatically create new instances at the right time
  • Smart notifications remind you when it matters most
  • Focus mode integration keeps distractions at bay

Intelligent Organization

Keep your tasks organized with powerful tagging and customization features that help you focus on what's important.

  • Custom tags and nested hierarchies for flexible organization
  • Pin important tags to the top for quick access
  • Customize colors and icons to make tasks visually distinct
  • Filter and search tasks by tags, dates, and status
  • Subtasks help break down complex projects

iOS Native Experience

Built specifically for Apple's ecosystem with deep integration and native design.

  • Native iOS widgets for quick task access
  • Focus mode integration to reduce distractions
  • Seamless iCloud sync across all your devices
  • Apple Watch companion app for quick task management
  • Designed with iOS design guidelines and accessibility

Why did I make TaskFrame?

I was frustrated with traditional to-do apps that showed me everything at once. I wanted an app that understands timing—showing me what matters right now, not everything I need to do eventually. TaskFrame was born from the belief that productivity isn't about managing more tasks, it's about managing them smarter.

The name "TaskFrame" reflects the app's core philosophy: framing your attention on the right tasks at the right time, rather than overwhelming you with everything at once.
