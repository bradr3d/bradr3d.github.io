This policy applies to all information collected or submitted in TaskFrame for iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and any other Apple platforms.

Overview

Privacy is a core value of TaskFrame. The app is designed to collect the minimum data necessary to make the experience great. Analytics are anonymized so they cannot be tied back to you, and your personal data is never sold.

What data we collect

Analytics

TaskFrame uses TelemetryDeck to collect basic, anonymized information about how the app is used. This may include:

  • High-level feature usage (e.g., which screens are opened or actions taken)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and basic runtime diagnostics (e.g., errors)

TelemetryDeck does not collect information that directly identifies you. Signals are pseudonymous and are not combined in a way that would identify who you are. Learn more in the TelemetryDeck privacy policy.

Payment data

Paid features and subscriptions are processed by Apple. TaskFrame uses RevenueCat to manage trials and subscriptions. Payment details are handled by Apple and are never shared with TaskFrame or RevenueCat. RevenueCat receives only anonymized identifiers necessary to manage your purchase entitlements. See the Apple privacy policy and the RevenueCat privacy policy.

Data synchronisation

Your data can sync across your devices using Appleâ€™s iCloud (CloudKit and iCloud Key-Value Store). Only the data required to power sync is stored in your private iCloud container, associated with your Apple ID, such as task content, metadata, and certain settings. This data is under your control in iCloud and is not accessible to TaskFrame.

Email

If you contact support, your email and the contents of your message will be retained to respond to your request. These emails are not used for marketing.

What we do with your data

Collected information is used to improve TaskFrame, diagnose issues, and provide support. Analytics are not used to identify you, and analytics data is not combined with other datasets in a way that would identify who you are.
